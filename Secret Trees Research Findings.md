# Secret Trees Research Findings

## **1. Hemp Carbon Verification**

### **EU-Recognized Methodologies**

- **EN 15804+A2** and **ISO 14067:2023** are the primary standards for quantifying biogenic carbon storage in construction materials[^4][^19].
- **EU Taxonomy Delegated Act 2023/2486** includes criteria for climate mitigation via carbon-negative building materials like hempcrete[^19].
- **CRCF Regulation (2024)** certifies carbon storage in buildings using hempcrete for ≥35 years[^20].


### **Certification Bodies**

- **TÜV SÜD**: €4,500–7,000 per verification[^4].
- **Bureau Veritas**: €3,800–6,200[^4].
- **Latvian National Accreditation Bureau (LATAK)**: €2,900[^4].


### **Scientific Validation**

- **RTU Study (2025)**: 19.4 tCO₂ sequestered per 25m² hempcrete structure in Baltic climates[^10].
- **EU Technical Assessment (2024)**: Requires hempcrete to demonstrate 23.8 tCO₂/25m² via EPDs[^19].

## **2. Regulatory Framework**

### **EU Building Codes**

- Hempcrete complies with **EU Construction Product Regulation** if THC <0.2%[^21].
- **Digital Product Passport (DPP)**: Required for all construction materials by 2026[^19].


### **Latvian Regulations**

- **Hemp Cultivation**: Permitted with EU-certified seeds (THC <0.2%)[^17][^21].
- **Zoning in Saldus**: Eco-tourism projects require:
    - 50m setback from protected forest areas[^9].
    - Use of ≥60% local materials (hemp, timber)[^9].

## **3. Technical Feasibility**

### **Baltic Climate Limitations**

- **Humidity Risk**: Hempcrete walls >30cm thick require vapor-permeable finishes to prevent mold[^10][^23].
- **Temperature Cycling**: RTU recommends adding 10% pozzolanic binder (metakaolin) for durability below -15°C[^10].

## **5. Token Legal \& Compliance**

### **EU Financial Regulations**

- **MiCA Compliance**: Tokens representing carbon storage qualify as utility tokens if no profit-sharing[^15][^16].
- **ESMA Guidelines**: Carbon tokens must link to verified EPDs and avoid "financial instrument" classification[^15].


### **Recommended Legal Structure**

- **DAO Foundation (Liechtenstein)**: Governs token issuance[^16].
- **Latvian UAB Subsidiary**: Manages physical assets[^16].
